Hello and welcome!

My name is Abby Doupnik and I'm a journalism student at the University of Florida. This site is an attempt to collect and showcase different projects and stories I have worked on in my time studying the art of storytelling. I feel that each projects I've worked on has fostered my passions for both writing and for public service. I dream of spending my life finding innovative ways to use media as a catalyst for social change and to inspire people to make a difference within their own communities.
